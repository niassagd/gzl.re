What is private lending?

Private lending is an alternative investment strategy where individuals act as lenders—like a bank issuing a mortgage—providing capital to real estate investors in exchange for fixed interest returns. These loans are secured and collateralizedby real estate assets, providing a predictable income stream while mitigating risk.
